Axis Capital

Axis Capital “Axis” is an investment platform with two funds providing capital to startups in Pakistan - 1) an Equity VC Fund (BitRate Fund) led by seasoned Venture Capitalist Faisal Aftab and 2) a Credit Fund led by ex-Goldman Sachs executive Faisal Chowdhry. Axis is considered to be the leading VC Fund in Pakistan and has a total AUM of $65M.
